Our Services

Workers United provides consulting, coordination, and support services related to international employment and work visa processes. We help connect workers and employers, guide both sides through documentation, and coordinate the process step by step.

We do not sell jobs and we do not guarantee job placement or visa approval. Final decisions are made by employers and relevant authorities.

Privacy Policy

What we collect. Information you submit through forms or email (name, email, phone if provided, country, message, and any documents you voluntarily send).
